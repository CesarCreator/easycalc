Welcome to simplecalc!

Available commands:

print("text") > Does the same as System.out.println, but simplified.
addwd(val1, val2) > Adds, but using doubles (decimal numbers)
add(val1, val2) > The classic addiction.
multiwd(val1, val2) > Multiply in doubles' mode.
multi(val1, val2) > The classic multiplication
minwd(val1, val2) > Subtracts in doubles' mode.
min(val1, val2) > The classic subtraction.
divwd(val1, val2) > Divide, in doubles' mode.
div(val1, val2) > The classic division
